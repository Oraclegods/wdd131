Description

Discover Nigeria is a website dedicated to showcasing the rich culture, history, and modern advancements of Nigeria. The name "Discover Nigeria" was chosen to reflect the site's mission of educating visitors about the country's diverse attractions, traditions, and innovations.

Optional Domain Availability: discover-nigeria.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Discover Nigeria is to provide an engaging and informative platform for people interested in learning about Nigeria’s culture, history, tourism, and economic growth. The website will feature:

  • Key historical and cultural landmarks
  • Popular tourist destinations and events
  • Insight into Nigeria’s economy and innovation sector
  • A gallery showcasing images of Nigeria’s people, landscapes, and festivals

Scenarios

  • "What are the top tourist attractions in Nigeria?"
  • "How has Nigeria’s tech industry evolved in recent years?"

Color Scheme

  • Green (#008000): Used for headings and buttons, representing Nigeria’s natural beauty and national identity.
  • Gold (#FFD700): Used for accents and highlights, symbolizing wealth and prosperity.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Background color for a clean and professional look.

Typography

  • Montserrat (Headings): Modern and bold font for section headings.
  • Open Sans (Body Text): Easy-to-read font for paragraphs and content.

Wireframe

Mobile View:

  • Logo & Navigation Menu (Hamburger icon)
  • Hero Image (Landmark or Festival)
  • Welcome Text & Quick Facts
  • Featured Articles (Grid format)
  • Footer (Social Media Links & Contact Info)

Desktop View:

  • Full-width Hero Image with Overlay Text
  • Navigation Bar (Home, Culture, Attractions, Economy, Contact)
  • Three Column Layout:
    • Left: Quick Facts & Recent Articles
    • Center: Featured Image & Main Content
    • Right: Sidebar with Links & Social Media
  • Footer with Contact Information & Newsletter Signup

CSS Support

  • External CSS file (styles.css) to maintain color scheme and typography.
  • Basic styling for consistent UI across different screen sizes.
  • Responsive design to ensure usability on mobile and desktop devices.

This site plan provides a structured approach to building an informative and visually appealing website about Nigeria.
